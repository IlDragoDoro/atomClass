William Shakespeare was the son of John Shakespeare, and alderman and a successfull glover originally from Snitterfield, and Mary Arden, the daughter of an adluent landrowing farmer. He was born in Stratford-upon-Avon and baptisted there on 26 April 1564. His actual date of birth remains uknown, but is traditionally observed on 23 April, Saint George's day. This date,wich can be traced back to an 18-century scholar's mistake, has proved appealing to biographers, because Shakespeare died on 23 April 1616[10] He was the third child of eight and the eldest surviving son.
